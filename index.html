Our Process

How We Fix
Your Load Cell

01

Receive & Diagnose

We perform a comprehensive electrical and mechanical diagnosis — checking bridge resistance, insulation, and physical damage to identify the exact failure point.

02

Precision Repair

Strain gauge replacement, micro-soldering, sealing, and structural repair carried out in our equipped workshop with specialist tools.

03

Calibrate & Certify

Multi-point calibration against certified standards. We verify output linearity, hysteresis, and repeatability before any unit leaves our facility.

04

Deliver & Support

Fast dispatch with calibration report. On-site re-installation support available. Warranty on all repaired units.
